# The Last Shift · Development (Stages 1–4) · 开发

## Stage 1 · Core dramatic action · 核心动作

```text
Protagonist: MEILING (50), night-shift clerk and owner of her late mother's store
Want: finish the closing inventory and hand over the keys at 06:00 without
      acknowledging KAI
Conflict: Kai—the father who left 38 years ago—breaks their three-month pact of
          silence on the final night and may leave before she can choose differently
Stakes/deadline: the store closes and the keys leave her hand at dawn
Logline: On her final night in her late mother's convenience store, a guarded clerk
         must finish the dawn handover without acknowledging the homeless regular
         she has secretly recognized as her father—until he says her name and turns
         to leave for good.
```

## Stage 2 · Synopsis · 梗概

> 美玲在母亲留下的便利店值最后一个夜班，清晨六点交钥匙。三个月来，她每天给流浪老人
> 凯留一份关东煮；她早已从他的旧伤和小动作认出这是离家三十八年的父亲，却始终隔着柜台
> 假装陌生。今晚，她贴出“明日六点交接”的告示。凯第一次叫出她的名字，又把母亲当年给
> 他的旧钥匙放在窗台。美玲把钥匙推回去，继续盘点。凯转身离开，卡住多年的门被阵风撞上，
> 她才发现自己保住的不是边界，而是一场永远不会结束的惩罚。天亮前，她回身重新盛了
> PROP-003，追出去递到他手里，再同他走回店门：不是原谅，只是第一次直接选择接触。清晨，
> 交接人在门外等；门从里面
> 关着，柜台上有两个空碗。
>
> Tone: quiet, cold-blue, restrained. Plot stays spare; behavior, objects, and
> thresholds carry the emotion.

## Stage 3 · Character & arc · 人物

```text
CHAR-001 · MEILING
  Want: complete inventory and handover without acknowledging Kai
  Need: stop confusing distance with safety; allow contact without pretending the
        past is repaired
  Arc: care delivered at a safe distance → one direct, bounded act of contact
  Contradiction: materially generous / relationally sealed
  Voice: short procedural sentences; answers facts, avoids names
  Behavior lock: cleans or counts when emotion threatens control

CHAR-002 · KAI · catalyst + mirror
  Want: see Meiling once more and return the old key without asking forgiveness
  Contradiction: returns nightly / refuses to cross the threshold
  Voice: sparse questions; never asks to be absolved
  Visual lock: thin coat, aluminum cans, pale burn scar on left wrist
```

## Stage 4 · Backstory, knowledge, and canon · 前史与正史

```text
Era/context: a fading neighborhood; the independent store has been sold and hands
  over at 06:00 after its final night shift.
Story clock: night of 2026-01-31 → handover at 06:00 on 2026-02-01.
Timeline: Kai left when Meiling was 12, 38 years ago. Her mother died in spring
  2025 ("去年" in SC004). Kai began appearing nightly three months ago.
Knowledge lock:
  - Meiling recognized Kai after his third visit from his wrist scar and coin-rub.
  - Kai recognized Meiling on the first night from her name badge and the store.
  - Neither acknowledged the other before SC004. SC002/SC003 plant the scar and
    coin-rub; SC004 makes the father/daughter truth unambiguous to the audience and
    turns their private recognition into spoken acknowledgment.
Relationship history: care has continued as a bowl left on the sill, never handed
  over. Kai has never stepped inside.
Location/prop canon:
  - LOC-001 store door has a dented floor spring; it sticks ~10 cm open until a
    strong gust knocks it shut.
  - PROP-001 is the nightly sill bowl; it spills in SC007 and is not reused.
  - PROP-002 is Kai's old store key, kept for 38 years.
  - PROP-003 is the replacement bowl Meiling hands Kai in SC009.
  - PROP-004 is Meiling's own untouched bowl; it becomes the second empty bowl in
    SC010 after the shared meal.
Cross-section: the final shift, when the 06:00 handover makes continued silence
  impossible.
```

Backstory is not dumped into dialogue. Only story-relevant facts become visible
through the notice, the scar/coin gesture, the old key, the stuck door, and the
changed handoff ritual.
