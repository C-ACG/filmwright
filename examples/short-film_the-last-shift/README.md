# Worked Vertical Slice · “The Last Shift” · 《最后一班》

An end-to-end **vertical slice**, not a finished short film: the example plans an
8-minute short through Stage 6, then carries pivotal scene `SC004` through
screenplay, shot breakdown, and QA.

这是一个端到端的“纵切片”范例，不冒充完整成片：先完成 8 分钟短片的场景地图，再把关键
场 `SC004` 完整推进到剧本、分镜和质检。

## Seed · 种子

> On the final night in her late mother's store, a clerk must decide whether to
> acknowledge the homeless regular she has recognized as the father who left 38
> years ago.
