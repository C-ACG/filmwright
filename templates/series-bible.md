# Template · Series Bible · 剧集圣经（季度规划 + 连续性追踪）

> A planning and continuity **view** projected from canonical Project State,
> produced in phase 1 and refreshed every episode. `templates/project-state.md`
> remains the single source of truth.
>
> Branch on `season arc?` before filling this view. With `no`, keep the editorial
> promise, episode map, and recurring continuity locks; mark the season synopsis,
> protagonist season arc, arc budget, hidden-line plan, and layered-finale release
> `N/A — pure anthology`. Do not invent them to fill the template.

## Discipline
- Phase 1 finishes the map and self-checks it (`series.md` workflow) before any
  episode is written.
- Merge the episode State Delta into Project State, then refresh C1–C4; read the
  current state/context pack before writing the next.
- On a continuity conflict, fix the new episode, not locked old ones.
- Checkpoint each episode (`memory-checkpoint.md`, series add-on).
